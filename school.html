The Thief lord

Cornelia Funke

Brief Summary

Characters List

Button

In this story, a detective is hired to find two children who had run off from their parents in Venis. The two kids meet some other kids. One of them is named the thief lord. He “steels” many items to get them money. Though not all was great, because the detective had found their hideout. The detective went into the base, and the kids sided him up as a prisoner. Later, the kids were presented with an offer. Find a magic wing for a huge amount of money. Before completing this mission, the other kids found out that this the lord lived in a mansion, and his parents were rich. Even though they did not like the thief lord as much, they stole the wing. After finding the wing, they followed the people they bought it from back to where they lived. There, they slept with the rats. They later were able to ride a marry go ride, which made the thief lord older.
