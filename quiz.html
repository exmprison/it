Step 1: Scrape the Page

The extension first acts as a virtual scanner. It efficiently scrapes all the visible text and interactive elements, such as buttons, checkboxes, and text fields, from the current webpage. This creates a detailed map of the content that needs to be analyzed.

Step 2: AI Analysis

Next, the scraped data is sent securely to Google's Gemini AI model. The AI processes the information, understands the questions, and determines the most likely correct answers. This step leverages the model's advanced reasoning and problem-solving capabilities to produce a highly accurate solution.

Step 3: Automation
